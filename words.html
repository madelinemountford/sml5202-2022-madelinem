Verbs and Nouns

Verbs are words that describe an action

Nouns are words that describe objects and things


In Spanish there are different ways to use verbs and nouns than in English


Spanish nouns must begin with el,la,las or los


Verbs in spanish end in -ar, -ir, or -er

We have to then add endings for the person you are talking about

This table shows the endings you need to use

-ar verbs -er or -ir verbs
I Hablo Come
You Hablas Comes
He/She Habla Come
We Hablamos Comemos
You Plural Hablais Comeis
They Hablan Comen
